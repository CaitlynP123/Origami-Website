The Camel

Some Facts About Camels

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two sets of eyelashes to keep the dust out
  2. Camels have thick lips that allow them to eat thorny plants that other animals can't
  3. Camels can drink up to 40 gallons in one go

The Chameleon

Some Facts About Chameleons

  1. Chameleons can move their eyes in opposite directions
  2. Chameleons change their skin color to communicate or regulate their body temperature
  3. Chameleons have very sticky spit

The Pigeon

Some Facts About Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are very sociable animals
  2. Pigeons are one of the first domesticated birds
  3. Dodos were related to Pigeons

The Teddy Bear

Some Facts About Bears

  1. Most bears have 42 teeth
  2. Bears have excellent sight, hearing and smell
  3. Bears grieve deeply for other bears

The Panda

Some Facts About Pandas

  1. An adult Panda can eat upto 12-38 kilos of bamboo per day
  2. An adult Panda weight more than 45 kilos
  3. Pandas can climb trees and are good at swimming

The Flying Cicada

Some Facts About Cicadas

  1. Some Cicada calls can be heard upto 1.5 miles away
  2. There are rougly 3,000 species of Cicadas
  3. Cicadas don't sting or bite
